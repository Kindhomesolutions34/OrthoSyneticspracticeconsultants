OrthoSynetics: Your Partner in Orthodontic Practice Success

In the dynamic world of orthodontic practice, a reliable and comprehensive support system is a game-changer. Enter OrthoSynetics, a pioneering force with over 30 years of experience, dedicated to ensuring the thriving success of orthodontic practices across the nation. Through their unwavering commitment to excellence, OrthoSynetics offers a complete suite of services that covers marketing, insurance verifications, collections, and procurement, making them an indispensable asset for any orthodontic practice.

Driving Excellence in Practice Growth

OrthoSynetics understands that practice growth requires a strategic approach, which is why they offer a full-service marketing solution. By crafting tailored marketing plans, conducting in-depth market research, refining brand identities, and deploying effective online strategies, OrthoSynetics helps practices gain a competitive edge and attract new patients. With OrthoSynetics as an orthodontic marketing partner, practices experience an impressive average increase of 35% in new patient appointments.

Efficient Collections for Steady Cash Flow

Maintaining a consistent cash flow is vital for practice sustainability, and OrthoSynetics excels in streamlining collections. Through efficient patient insurance verification, receivables management, and a well-organized collections process, OrthoSynetics ensures practices receive the reimbursements they deserve promptly. Backed by a robust insurance benefits and eligibility verification process, the dedicated team at OrthoSynetics has achieved an impressive average collection rate of 98%, handling over $1.3 billion in practice collections.

Optimizing Procurement for Savings

OrthoSynetics extends its expertise beyond patient interactions to help practices optimize their procurement processes. With access to a 24/7 online ordering system and the ability to order from multiple vendors simultaneously, practices benefit from cost-effective equipment and office supplies procurement. By securing discounted prices without the need for bulk orders, OrthoSynetics empowers practices to save significantly. On average, OrthoSynetics clients save an impressive 25% on brackets and appliances.

Endorsement from a Satisfied Practitioner

Dr. John Andrews of Andrews Orthodontics attests to OrthoSynetics' effectiveness in elevating practice success. In a world where orthodontic practices face multifaceted challenges, OrthoSynetics stands out as a dedicated partner committed to fostering growth, efficiency, and success. With a rich history of building practices and a steadfast focus on providing unmatched solutions, OrthoSynetics continues to be the not-so-secret advantage that orthodontic practices can rely on.
